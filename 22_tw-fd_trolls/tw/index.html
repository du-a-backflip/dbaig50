ALL ABOUT STARS!

What is a Star?

Stars are hot balls of gas, that generate energy and glow! They are neat. And they're very pretty. Usually they're made of hydrogen and helium!

What kinds of Stars Are there?

There are lots of different kinds of stars! They can be different sizes and colors and some burn hotter than others! There's dwarf stars (red, brown, yellow, black, white) and beetleguses and red giants and blue giants and protostars.


The Sun!

The Sun is a yellow dwarf star! It's the star the Earth orbits! It's part of the Milky Way
